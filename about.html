My story

My academic and career experiences are unique as my personal story. Language schools in Berlin and London, architecture university in Venice and Vienna and architecture jobs in Vienna and Wetzikon. Followed by a remote UX Design certification. Each of these experiences was influenced by a childhood that began on the sea in Pula.

When I was a kid, in Croatia there were very few toys to buy and I've learned to design everything by myself. Growing up, I learned how to design experiences through accessories, products, buildings, touristic tours before landing in digital space.

There were plenty of challenges during this time that made me master skills such as empathy, open mindness, adaptation, active listening, team work, problem solving and leading. Moreover, all these challenges had a great impact on me as an individual. They taught me to value and cherish a diversity of opinions and perspectives when developing ideas into innovations — we need more user centered designs in our lives.

In a variety of situations, I have watched digitalisation change the way we work, shop, think and experience the world. I am proud to be a UX Designer and therefore invited to shape a digital future as human centered. My belief is that great products are the result of diverse and creative teams who love observing the world, learning and finding solutions while meeting the client’s needs.
